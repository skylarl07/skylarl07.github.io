About Me

Hi, I'm a third-year undergraduate student pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Mathematics at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (or Virginia Tech).
I am currently working as a research assistant for CSGenome, a research group under the Department of Computer Science, where I help testing and developing the database API, and create SQLAlchemy models for incoming data. In addition, I'm working on a undergraduate research project related to data freshness. Besides that, I'm also a teaching assistant for the Department of Mathematics, where I mainly help with multivariable calculus related courses.
I am passionate about many things related and not related to computer science, as you can see from the hobbies section. I dream of becoming a video game developer, and create outstanding games like one of my favorite of all time, the Uncharted series.
